Research Statement

My Research uses statistical signal processing techniques on human brain images to draw inferences of affective/Social-emotional processing. Currently developing a mechanistic model for longitudinal morphological changes of gray matter, I analyze data from a longitudinal community sample from youth ages 14-19 who attend schools in urban Los Angeles Unified School District.
